Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our digital healthcare platform

How secure are digital consultations?

All consultations are encrypted end-to-end and comply with international healthcare data protection standards. Your medical information remains completely confidential and secure.

How do digital prescriptions work?

Doctors create digitally signed prescriptions that are instantly shared with verified pharmacies. You receive a unique code to collect your medication, eliminating prescription fraud and ensuring authenticity.

Is my data protected?

Yes, we adhere to strict data protection regulations including HIPAA and GDPR equivalent standards. Your medical records are encrypted and accessible only to authorized healthcare providers.

Are all doctors verified?

Absolutely. Every healthcare provider undergoes rigorous verification including medical license validation, specialization certification, and background checks before joining our platform.

What's the average response time?

Most consultations are connected within 15 minutes. For urgent cases, we have priority routing that connects you with available doctors in under 5 minutes, 24/7.

What payment methods are accepted?

We accept all major payment methods including bank transfers, debit/credit cards, and mobile money. We're also integrated with major Nigerian health insurance providers.

Still have questions?

Our support team is available 24/7 to help you with any questions about our platform.
